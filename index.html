Crystal Collector

How to Play

  • The Crytsals contain random numbers.
  • Each time you click on any one of 4 choices available, you add to your total score
  • The objective is to use combination of clicks to march the Target score displayed on top of crytsals
  • You win when your combination matches the target score, and you loss if your combination exceeds the target score.
  • Good luck as you match your way to the jacket.
